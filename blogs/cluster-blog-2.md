## The Anatomy of a Perfect Flaky Croissant

Crafting a premium croissant requires extreme patience, strict temperature control, and a commitment to classical methods. Our dedicated culinary team bakes daily to deliver what critics rate among the **best bakery items in Patna**.

Step 1: Extended Cold Sourdough Fermentation (Deepens dough flavor complexity)
Step 2: Micro-Layering Pure Quality Butter (Creates the iconic pastry lift)
Step 3: High-Heat Stone Deck Baking (Achieves the shattering golden crust)
The result is unmistakable. When you pull one of our fresh-baked croissants apart, you don't find dry crumbs. You are greeted by a beautiful, steaming aerated honeycomb structure that smells of rich butter.
