## What Exactly is Specialty Coffee?

Most commercial chains prioritize shelf-life over sensory character. They mass-roast low-grade beans until they are completely charred, wiping out the delicate flavors of the land. 

Specialty coffee treats farming like a fine art. To earn this title, coffee must score above 80 points on a strict 100-point scale regulated by the Specialty Coffee Association.

[ Commercial Coffee ] -> Mass-produced, bitter profile, dark over-roasting.
[ Specialty Coffee  ] -> Premium Arabica, single-origin tracking, notes of caramel/citrus.


At Brew Haven Café, every single shot pulled is a lesson in precision. We work with premium Arabica lots from tracked estates, roasting them carefully to preserve natural notes of smooth caramel, deep chocolate, and bright citrus.
