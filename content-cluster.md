## 🔗 Internal Linking Intercept Matrix

### 1. Pillar Page Links out to Spokes
*   Within the workspace section of the Pillar Hub, hyper-link out to `cluster-blog-1.md` using the exact anchor text: **"work-friendly cafes in Patna"**.
*   Within the pastry craft section of the Pillar Hub, hyper-link out to `cluster-blog-2.md` using the exact anchor text: **"best bakery items in Patna"**.

### 2. Spoke Pages Link back to Pillar Hub
*   Every standalone cluster spoke article MUST contain a contextual link in its introductory or closing paragraph pointing back to `pillar-blog.md` using the exact anchor text: **"specialty coffee in Patna"**. This structural loop secures page authority.
